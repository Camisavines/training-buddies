  • Workout 1
    Workout

    Begin with 200 to 400 meters of easy swimming, and then do the following two drills to warm up for your swimming workout. Kicks: Grab a kickboard and hold onto it as you swim 100 meters. Rest 20 seconds after each effort. Repeat four times total.

    Source: https://www.beachbodyondemand.com/blog/must-do-swim-workouts

  • Workout 2
    Workout

    This is a harder swimming workout.

    1. 200 free
    2. 200 IM drill.
    3. 8 x 100 free (15 sec rest)
    4. 8 x 50 breaststroke pull with dolphin kick.
    5. 2 x 100 back 6 kick switch.
    6. 400 choice/free.
    7. 4 x 50 (25 hard, 25 easy)
    8. Cooldown

    Source: www.restonmasters.com/resources/workouts/

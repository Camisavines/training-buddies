  • Workout 1
    Confined Dribbling

    This drill helps you dribble fast and accurately in a tight space while avoiding a defender.

    1. Set up four cones in a box 5 yards apart.
    2. Stand in the box with a ball and a partner.
    3. Dribble for the specified time while your partner tries to steal the ball.
  • Workout 2
    Partner/Wall Drill

    This drill helps you control the ball when you receive a pass. It also reinforceyour technique.

    1. Have a partner toss a ball in the air or bounce it off a wall
    2. Pass back and forth 10-20 times, using the inside of your foot.
  • Workout 3
    Closed-Space Dribbling

    This helps you use all different surfaces of your feet.

    1. Place three cones on the ground in a triangle or line.
    2. Dribble the ball between the cones, using all surfaces of your feet.
    3. Perform quick touches to guide the ball around the cones.
  • Workout 4
    Running

    This drill helps you control the ball when you receive a pass. It also reinforces your technique.

    This helps you become faster and have a more stroner shot.

    Running around cones can help increase stamina.

  • Workout 5
    One Touch Passing

    This helps your touch become better and more controlled.

    1. Players pass and follow one touch but the servers remain in their places.
  • Workout 6
    Cone Drill

    This drill increases your ability to trap the ball on the inside or outside of your foot and keep it away from defenders.

    1. Line up six to 10 cones 5 yards apart.
    2. Weave through the cones, dribbling the ball with the inside of your feet.

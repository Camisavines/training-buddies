  • Workout 1
    Flying 40s to Enhance Muscular Endurance

    This workout helps you build power and train your body to recover quickly between efforts for events that demand repeated surges.

    1. In a medium to large gear, push hard for 40 seconds.
    2. Recover 20 seconds.
    3. Repeat 10 times.
    4. Do up to four, resting five minutes between sets.

      5. Source: https://www.bicycling.com/training/g20027530/quick-cycling-workouts-for-power-and-endurance/?slide=1

  • Workout 2
    10-Speeds to Improve Pedaling Efficiency

    These lightning-fast efforts help you develop a fluid and efficient cadence

    1. Pedal as hard as you can for 10 seconds in a gear you can push 90 to 110 rpm with effort.
    2. Then spin easy for 20 seconds.
    3. Repeat for 10 to 15 minutes
    4. Rest five minutes. Then do another set.

      5. Source: https://www.bicycling.com/training/g20027530/quick-cycling-workouts-for-power-and-endurance/?slide=1

  • Workout 3
    Hill Charges

    This helps you ride up hills. Eventually hills wont be such a big obstacle.

    1. On a moderate incline, stand up on the pedals and charge up the hill as fast as possible for 30 seconds.
    2. Coast back to your starting point and repeat, this time seated.
    3. Alternate between standing and sitting for six climbs.
    4. Recover 10 minutes then do another set.

      5. Source: https://www.bicycling.com/training/g20027530/quick-cycling-workouts-for-power-and-endurance/?slide=1
